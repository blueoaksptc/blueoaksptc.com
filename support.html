Partners

The Blue Oaks PTC does not have a “one size fits all” fundraising program.  Instead, we try to offer a selection for our families to pick and choose. We rely on the following programs to help us fund the programs, services, and activities that are vital to the students at Blue Oaks.

Join us in our easy year-long partnership with Round Table. Special Family Fundraiser Cards were sent home with each student. The offer on the card is: One large, 2-topping pizza, 6 twists, 4 drinks OR a 2-liter for only $17.99. Each time you use this card at the 2 locations listed (dine in, take out or delivery), Round Table will donate $2 to Blue Oaks PTC. The cards have an expiration date of June 30, 2011. For more information, see the flyer that was sent home.

Simply clip and turn in your 10¢ Box Tops coupons to your teacher or the school office and you’ve helped the PTC earn money for the school.  For more information about the Box Tops for Education program or to see a list of participating products, visit www.boxtops4education.com.

Click here for more information on Box Tops (.pdf).

eScrip is a fundraising program where participating retail partners contribute a percentage of your grocery loyalty cards, credit card, and debit/ATM card purchases to the PTC. Visit www.escrip.com for more information about the eScrip program, to see a complete list of participating retailers, and to sign up for the program.

The Parent Teacher Club is excited to introduce the Campbell’s Labels for Education program to Blue Oaks. This program is a great way to get FREE merchandise for our school by collecting points from the Campbell’s products you buy every day.

Check your product labels and caps for the above Labels for Education logo. For labels, clip out the logo with UPC portion and turn them in. For products with caps, just turn in the caps. Please submit items either in an envelope, or paper or plastic bag (no staples, tape or glue please!) You can either send them to class with your child or deposit them into the collection bin in the front office. That’s all there is to it! Read the Campbell's Labels for Education flyer (.pdf) for more information.

Mark-My-Time has a digital bookmark making it easier for students to keep track of daily reading minutes.  If you are interested in purchasing one of these bookmarks for your student, you can order them online at www.mark-my-time.com Blue Oaks PTC will receive a 20% donation for online orders.  Please enter CA181E1 in the School Promotion code area.

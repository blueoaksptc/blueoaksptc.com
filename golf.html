The Blue Oaks Golf Tournament and Parent Social is not only a fun-filled afternoon and evening, but also a wonderful opportunity for our community to come together in support of our school.

We are excited and looking forward to our first annual golf tournament in April 2015.

Thank you to all of you who have continued to support Blue Oaks Elementary school.

Prizes will be awarded for:

  • Hole-in-One
  • Closest to the Pin
  • Long Drive
  • 1st, 2nd and 3rd Place
We hope to see you on the course!

Entry Fee

US $100.00 per player* (includes: green fees, cart, catered dinner and prizes)

Four Person Team

Each team will consist of 4 players.

If you do not enter a team list on the entry form, then you will be placed with others to create a team.

Procedure

Men will play from the blue tees; women from the red tees. Each team member will tee off and the captain will determine the ball with the best position; all team members will play their second shot from that point. Repeat the procedure until the ball is holed out.

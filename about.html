About Us

PTC Board Members   |   PTC Budget   |   PTC Bylaws

Blue Oaks Parent Teacher Club (PTC) is a non-profit, volunteer organization established to benefit the students at Blue Oaks Elementary School.

The goal of the PTC is to bring parents, teachers, and staff members together to improve and enhance our children's school experience. We are able to achieve this goal by:
  • Volunteering for school events and projects
  • Raising funds for a variety of school needs
  • Sponsoring fun school and family activities and events
  • Providing a variety of resource support for teachers
  • Promoting open communication between parents and staff

PTC Budget

Every year, parents provide input on the Site Council's annual survey about programs, activities, and services they would like to support at Blue Oaks. Next, the PTC takes those results and determines the cost to fund them. Then, we plan our fundraisers to meet those funding numbers.

Some of the programs, services, and activities the Blue Oaks PTC supports include:
  • Classroom Supplies
  • Library Books
  • Enrichment Programs
  • Assemblies
